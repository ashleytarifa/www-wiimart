FAQ

What is WiiMart?

WiiMart is a revival project, replacing the original Wii Shop Channel. You can download Virtual Console, WiiWare, and Channels for your Nintendo Wii.

How do I install WiiMart?

To install WiiMart, please follow the detailed step-by-step instructions in the installation guide section above. You will need a modded Wii (or vWii) and an SD card. If you're new to modding, we recommend researching how to safely mod your console before proceeding.

Is it safe?

Yes! WiiMart is completely safe to use. It doesn't require any personal information, and we ensure all downloads are free from malware. However, please note that using WiiMart requires a modded Wii (or vWii). Modding your console comes with some risks, so ensure you're following reliable guides and procedures.

Will this fully replace the Wii Shop?

Yes, since the original Wii Shop Channel has been unsupported by Nintendo since 2019, WiiMart is a complete replacement for it.

Will Homebrew games and apps be added?

Not our main focus right now. We're focusing on supporting the original Wii titles and preserving the best of the Wii Shop experience.

What is the estimated timeline?

The timeline for updates and new features is continuously evolving. You can check the current status, including any upcoming releases or expected features, by clicking here.

What is error code XXXXXX?

If you encounter an error, you can find a list of common WiiMart error codes and their solutions here. If your issue isn't listed, feel free to reach out for further support.

How can I get support if I have issues?

If you encounter any problems or have questions about using WiiMart, feel free to contact our support team at our Discord Server.
