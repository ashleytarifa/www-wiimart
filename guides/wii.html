Wii / vWii Installation Guide

You need to have a modded Wii or vWii to use the WiiMart revival. If your Wii isn't modded yet, follow the guides below to mod it:

  • Modding your Wii
  • Modding your vWii

Requirements:

  1. A modded Wii or vWii.
  2. An SD Card.
  3. WiiMart WAD and patched IOS WAD for your console (Wii/vWii).
  4. A WAD Manager (recommended: YAWMME and Any Title Deleter MOD).

Installation Guide:

    1. Prepare the SD Card:

  • Insert your SD Card into your computer.
  • Download the WiiMart WAD and the patched IOS WAD for your console (Wii or vWii).
  • Place both WAD files inside the "wad" folder on your SD Card.

    2. Install WAD Managers:

  • Download YAWMME (a WAD Manager for installing WADs) from here.
  • Download Any Title Deleter MOD from here.
  • Place both folders inside the "apps" folder on your SD Card.

    3. Transfer Files to the Wii:

  • Eject your SD Card from your computer and insert it into your Wii.
  • Turn on your Wii and launch The Homebrew Channel.
  • Open Any Title Deleter MOD through The Homebrew Channel.

    4. Uninstall the Original Wii Shop Channel:

  • If prompted, select IOS249 to load and press A on your Wii Remote.
  • Using the D-Pad, navigate through System Channel Titles and select Wii Shop Channel by pressing A.
  • Press A to uninstall the Wii Shop Channel.
  • Wait for the uninstallation to finish. Once done, press any key, and then press HOME to exit Any Title Deleter MOD.

    5. Install the Patched IOS

  • Open YAWMME from the Homebrew Channel.
  • Select Wii SD Slot as your source device and press A.
  • Using the D-Pad, navigate to IOS56 and press A to install it.
  • Press A again to confirm the installation and wait for it to finish.
  • After installation, press any key to continue.

    6. Install WiiMart:

  • Repeat the previous step for the WiiMart.WAD file.
  • After installation, press HOME to exit YAWMME.

    7. Launch WiiMart:

  • Go back to your Wii Menu and launch WiiMart from the system menu.

    Troubleshooting:

  • Error -1022 or -1029:

If you receive an error such as -1022 or -1029 when trying to launch WiiMart, it means you still have the original Wii Shop Channel installed.

  • Use Any Title Deleter MOD to delete the original Wii Shop Channel.
  • After deleting it, repeat the installation process for the WAD files.
